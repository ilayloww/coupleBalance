# Couple Balance

Simplify shared expenses and keep your relationship balanced.

**Couple Balance** is a modern, intuitive expense tracker designed specifically for couples. Forget complicated spreadsheets or generic group splitting apps; Couple Balance focuses on the 1-on-1 dynamic, letting you quickly log expenses, see who paid for what, and keep a running total of your shared balance effortlessly.

## Features

- **1-on-1 Expense Tracking**: Tailored for couples to manage shared finances.
- **Real-time Balance**: Instantly see who owes whom and how much.
- **Flexible Splitting**:
  - **Equal Split**: Standard 50/50 sharing.
  - **Full Amount**: One person pays the full cost.
  - **Custom Split**: Split by specific percentage or exact amounts.
- **Settlement History**: Archive past transactions and keep track of settled balances.
- **Smart Categories**: Quick tags for Food, Coffee, Groceries, Rent, and more.
- **Receipt Attachments**: Add photos of receipts to transactions.
- **Partner Linking**: Securely link accounts with your partner.
- **Multi-language Support**: Available in English and Turkish.

## Getting Started

1.  **Clone the repository**
2.  **Install dependencies**: `flutter pub get`
3.  **Setup Firebase**: Ensure you have `google-services.json` (Android) and `GoogleService-Info.plist` (iOS) configured.
4.  **Run the app**: `flutter run`

## Tech Stack

- **Flutter**: Cross-platform mobile development.
- **Firebase**: Backend for Authentication, Firestore, and Storage.
- **Provider**: State management.
